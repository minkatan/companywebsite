Meet the Founder

With over 20 years of experience in healthcare finance, our founder has served as CFO across multiple growth-stage and investor-backed organizations — from specialty care groups and diagnostics labs to digital health startups and CROs.

He’s led teams through everything from first-time audits to $100M exits, built finance functions from scratch, and translated complex operations into investor-ready metrics. His focus: give leaders better visibility, more confidence, and real control over their numbers.

After years of seeing founders get lost in financial ambiguity, he launched this firm to offer the kind of hands-on CFO support that drives strategic decisions — without the full-time headcount.

Today, he partners with visionary operators across North America, turning messy data into momentum — and balance sheets into boardroom trust.
